    \textbf{ Problem 3.10 (a):} Find Z tranform of $\dfrac{1}{2}^{n}u[n]$, also find the region of convergence.\\
\solution The Z -transform of x[n] is defined as ,
\begin{align}
X[n]&=\sum_{0}^{\infty}x[n]z^{-n}\\
&=\sum_{0}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{2}^{n}z^{-n}\\
&=\sum_{0}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{2z}^{n}\\
&=\dfrac{1}{1-\dfrac{1}{2z}}\\
&=\dfrac{1}{1-\dfrac{z^{-1}}{2}}
\end{align}
 The region of convergence is $|z|>\dfrac{1}{2}$
